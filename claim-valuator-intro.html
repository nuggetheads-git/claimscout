Comprehensive Factor Analysis

We consider multiple data points to build our valuation.

Location & Access

Evaluate the claim's geographical quality and ease of access, from landlocked parcels to road-adjacent sites.

Filing & History

Consider the claim's age, as recent filings can carry different risks and opportunities than older ones.

Data & Experience

Factor in the availability of assay data and the buyer's own experience level, which can heavily influence a claim's potential.

Travel Costs

Account for fuel expenses and potential vehicle maintenance costs associated with accessing and working the claim.
