Detector Pricing Guide

This goal of this page is to provide a risk assessment framework when considering a purchase of any of these models.

* Indicates an obsolete detector based on manufacturer provided data that is no longer supported by its manufacturer. Price based on stock accessories. Additional accessories may add additional value as a package sale. Prices are based on author's experience and opinion only. The author uses and recommends Minelab, White's, Garrett, and Fisher products. Note: Minelab's commitment to transparency shines through in their promise to support older products for at least five years after discontinuation, ensuring customers are informed about repair challenges when parts become scarce.
